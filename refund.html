Refund Policy

Last updated: June 7, 2026

We want you to be happy with KeebKlean. If it isn't right for you, we offer a simple, no-hassle refund.

14-day money-back guarantee

If you purchased KeebKlean as a direct download and you're not satisfied for any reason, email us within 14 days of purchase and we'll issue a full refund — no questions asked.

How to request a refund

Email support@keebklean.com with the email address you used at checkout (and your order number if you have it). Refunds are processed by Paddle.com, our Merchant of Record for direct purchases, back to your original payment method. Please allow a few business days for the refund to appear.

Mac App Store purchases

If you bought KeebKlean through the Apple Mac App Store, refunds are handled by Apple, not by us. Request a refund through Apple at reportaproblem.apple.com.

Questions

Not sure which version you have, or having trouble? Just email support@keebklean.com and we'll help.
